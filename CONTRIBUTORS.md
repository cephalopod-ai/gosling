# Contributors

Gosling is independently maintained from its upstream source project, [goose](https://github.com/aaif-goose/goose).

GitHub's contributor graph is calculated from commit history. Because Gosling preserves the
upstream history required for authorship and license attribution, that graph includes people who
contributed to goose before Gosling became an independent project. Detaching the repository from
GitHub's fork network does not remove those commit authors.

Gosling-specific development begins after the independent-fork baseline
[`c5fbbd7087c6d3a38259ed797f3b5a0c2333470a`](https://github.com/cephalopod-ai/gosling/commit/c5fbbd7087c6d3a38259ed797f3b5a0c2333470a).
The current post-fork contribution history is available in the
[GitHub comparison](https://github.com/cephalopod-ai/gosling/compare/c5fbbd7087c6d3a38259ed797f3b5a0c2333470a...main).

Upstream authors retain credit through the preserved Git history, Apache 2.0 license, and the
provenance notice in the [README](README.md). Their presence in GitHub's contributor graph does not
imply that they maintain, support, or endorse Gosling.
